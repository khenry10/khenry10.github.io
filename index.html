About Me

As a Product Manager at a start-up I worked closely with the Software Engineers, and I found myself envious of them. I wanted to have a bigger impact beyond creating user stories, crafting technical requirements, wireframes, creating new processes and making the business case. My relentless desire to code, build and create overcame my fear of the unknown. As someone who loves to push himself, I knew I would regret not taking a risk. The challenge and perseverance to overcome has been an amazing part of the GA experience, second only to my rapid and continuous learning.

When I'm not coding I like to stay active. I've run two marathons and have had loose plans to run another, for the past 4 years... I am really interested in the 'quantified self' movement, self knowledge through numbers, so I'm always looking for new life hacks to make everday life easier.
